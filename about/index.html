A bullet journal, often abbreviated as “BuJo,” is a versatile and customizable organizational system. It combines elements of a planner, to-do list, and diary into one cohesive notebook. The main idea behind bullet journaling is to streamline your life by organizing tasks, events, notes, and goals in a structured yet flexible format.
