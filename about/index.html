About

Quick Details

Hi there! I am a software engineer from China. Since my childhood, I have had a deep love for reading, and reading in bookstores is one of my fondest memories. Growing up in a small town, I was filled with curiosity about the vast world. Reading allowed my thoughts to transcend time and space. To me, imagination signifies freedom, and writing is the best conduit for imagination, also a lifelong dream of mine. As there’s a saying in China, “As I see it, the flowers take on my colors.” I hope my words can create ripples in people’s hearts at some point, that would make me happy.
